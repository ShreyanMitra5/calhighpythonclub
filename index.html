F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When are club meetings?

    Club meetings are bi-weekly on Thursday at lunchtime. More details like exact dates, will be sent out later by email.

  • How long are club meetings?

    Club meetings are usually the whole lunch period, or about 30 mins.

  • When will the content of the meeting be posted?

    Expect any form of content to be posted right after the meeting or at the end of the day, by email and it will be published on the website. Questions about how to access it, will be answered in the emails.

  • What is the curriculum?

    Please head to the header, and click More!, next click on Syllabus.

  • Do I need to bring or pay anything to attend this meeting?

    No! This club meeting does not have any type of club dues, and all we expect you to bring is your fully charged chromebook!

  • Who do I contact if I have any more questions?

    Please contact calhighpythonclub@gmail.com if you have any further questions.
